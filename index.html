About me

Hello, I'm Hojoon Yoon, from Oregon State University with a major in New Media Communication/Digital Communication Art. My educational journey has equipped me with a diverse skill set, having delved into Sound Design, UX/UI Design, Media Entrepreneurship and Management, Motion Design, and 3D Design. Throughout my academic tenure, I honed my proficiency in industry-standard design programs such as Adobe, Maya, and Unity.

As an active member of the Like Lion Club, I embraced the opportunity to learn to code and collaborate on impactful group CS projects. Notably, I contributed to developing a lunch menu-choosing website, showcasing my expertise in front-end development. My passion lies at the intersection of creativity and technology. I am eager to bring my skills and enthusiasm to dynamic projects that push the boundaries of design and innovation. Explore my portfolio to witness the fusion of my education and experiences.
